# 🎓 E-Learning Platform Purchase Analytics (MySQL)

🚀 **Project Summary**  
This project explores purchase behavior on an e-learning platform using MySQL. By analyzing learner activity, course performance, and transaction data, the project delivers meaningful insights into revenue generation, category demand, and customer engagement.

The analysis is designed to simulate real-world business scenarios where organizations need to optimize course offerings, pricing strategies, and marketing efforts using data-driven decisions.

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview
- **Database:** Learning  
- **Tables:** Learners, Courses, Purchases  
- **Learners:** 5  
- **Courses:** 6  
- **Transactions:** 8 purchase records  
- **Analysis Period:** January 2025  

This structured relational dataset enables multi-dimensional analysis across learners, courses, and purchasing patterns.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals
- Understand learner purchasing behavior  
- Identify top-performing courses and categories  
- Measure revenue contribution across categories  
- Detect high-value learners and engagement patterns  
- Identify gaps such as unpurchased or underperforming courses  

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights for e-learning platforms.

---

| Metric | Value |

The platform shows strong engagement with all learners making at least one purchase and most courses receiving demand.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue & Category Performance
- The **Data category** is the highest revenue contributor, generating over **44% of total revenue**  
- Programming and Marketing categories follow, showing diversified learning demand  
- Premium courses contribute more revenue per transaction, while lower-priced courses drive volume  

---

### 📘 Course Demand Trends
- **Digital Marketing Basics** is the most purchased course by quantity  
- **Machine Learning 101** shows strong performance in both demand and revenue  
- One course (**Web Development Basics**) recorded **zero purchases**, indicating a potential pricing or positioning issue  

---

### 👥 Learner Behavior Analysis
- All learners made at least one purchase → **100% conversion rate**  
- Some learners purchased across multiple categories → showing **cross-domain learning interest**  
- Higher spending is observed among learners exploring multiple skill areas  

---

### 🌍 Geographic Insights
- Learners are distributed across **India, USA, UK, and Spain**  
- Indian learners show higher overall spending patterns  
- Geographic diversity suggests global demand for online learning  

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- **Data-related courses are in highest demand**, aligning with current industry trends  
- **Course pricing impacts volume vs revenue trade-off**  
- **Multi-category learners indicate opportunity for bundling strategies**  
- **Unpurchased courses highlight content or pricing gaps**  

This analysis provides a foundation for improving both product strategy and customer engagement.

---

## 📌 Strategic Recommendations
- Expand **Data and Analytics course offerings**  
- Re-evaluate pricing or marketing strategy for underperforming courses  
- Introduce **bundle packages** to increase cross-category purchases  
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions  
- Use learner behavior insights to personalize course recommendations
